Debug

Struggling with JavaScript bugs? Looking for test guidance? Need help with legacy compatibility? Our engineers are passionate about making the web work for all our mutual customers, and we want to help. Grab a drink at the Debug Bar and we'll take a look!

Performance

Web performance is hard but we can help! Show us your site and we'll apply the same tools we use to build our advance applications. Our team has years of experiencing helping properties around the world identify and remediate performance pitfalls across browsers, so your customers have a better experience on every device. We'll provide actionable recommendations for your site, and opportunities to follow up with a deeper conversation with our developers.

Security

New security tools are here! Learn the new specification and how to apply them to make your web apps hack proof. Come try out live attack lab and see how your site holds uo. Learn more about building sites which incorporate authentication and authorization on both client and server and see how you can get started developing the most secure apps on the planet- no previous experience required!

And More

That's just the beginnning! We are partnering with teams across the industry to deliver opportunities for trying out new experiences and learn about new tools. We'll have representatives from GitHub, Visual Studio, TypeScript, the brand-new .NET Core 2.0 framework, and more.
